Fahrenheit to Celcius

How To Convert Form Celcius (oC) To Fahrenheit (oF)

The temperature in degrees Fahrenheit is equal to the temperature in degrees Celsius times 9/5 plus 32.

S(oF) = (S(oC) × 9/5) + 32

or

S(oF) = (S(oC) × 1.8) + 32
